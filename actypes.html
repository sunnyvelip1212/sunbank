🌞 Sun Bank Account Types

At Sun Bank, we believe in offering the right account for every stage of your life. Whether you're saving for the future, managing your daily finances, or planning for retirement, we have an account tailored just for you.

🏦 Regular Savings Account

Ideal for: Everyday banking needs

  • Minimum balance: ₹1,000
  • Interest Rate: 3.25% p.a.
  • Free passbook & ATM card
  • Internet & mobile banking enabled

💎 Sun Premium Savings Account

Ideal for: Customers seeking premium features

  • Minimum balance: ₹10,000
  • Interest Rate: 4.00% p.a.
  • Higher ATM withdrawal limits
  • Priority customer service
  • Cashback on select debit card purchases

👵 Senior Citizen Savings Account

Ideal for: Individuals aged 60 and above

  • Zero balance account
  • Interest Rate: 4.50% p.a.
  • Monthly interest credit
  • Free SMS & cheque book services
  • Higher Fixed Deposit interest rates

🎓 Student Savings Account

Ideal for: Students aged 18–25

  • No minimum balance
  • Interest Rate: 3.75% p.a.
  • Free digital banking services
  • Educational offers & discounts
  • ATM card with spending control

📈 Fixed Deposit (FD) Account

Ideal for: Long-term savings with guaranteed returns

  • Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
  • Interest Rate: Up to 7.25% p.a.
  • Premature withdrawal available
  • Senior citizen benefits

📝 Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Proof of address (Electricity bill, Rent Agreement, etc.)
