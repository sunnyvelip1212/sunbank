About SUN BANK

Welcome to SUN BANK

SUN BANK is a trusted name in modern banking, founded in 2010 with the mission to bring affordable, digital, and accessible banking services to every corner of India.

How We Achieved This

  • Started with a single branch in Panaji, Goa
  • Expanded to 200+ branches nationwide by 2020
  • Adopted full digital banking in 2015
  • Launched mobile banking and zero balance accounts for rural outreach
  • Partnered with over 50 government schemes for direct transfers

Our Vision

To empower every citizen with safe, smart, and sustainable financial services. We strive to be the most people-friendly bank in India, focusing on inclusion, innovation, and impact.
